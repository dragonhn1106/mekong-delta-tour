Highlights

  • Only 2 hours on a brand new highway from Hanoi to Hai Phong city - Very fast & comfortable.
  • Explore an unique and less touristy route in Lan Ha Bay - Off the beaten track!
  • Enjoy super fun activities likes a lesson on how to fish using nets, kayak
  • around the bay, and take a boat to explore caves.
  • Stay in luxury amenities with a comfortable bed and big private balcony.
  • Enjoy a wide sundeck offers a ton of light & incredible views.

Itineraries

Day 1: Hanoi - Halong (L,D)

06:00 - 08:00: join the Tai Chi class on the sundeck to feel the fresh air and refresh your body to start a new day. After that the crew serve breakfast with coffee and tea in the dining room.
08:00 - 11:30: get on tender to visit the Sung Sot Cave, one of the biggest caves in Halong Bay with amazing stalactites and stalagmites. Prepare your camera to capture great moments of your journey.

We recommend you to wear comfortable shoes.

Getting back to the Pelican Cruise, time to check out and settle the bill of services that you used on board (drinks, massage, etc.) at reception. Lunch is served while the cruise sails back to Tuan Chau port. You can relax on the sundeck or in the dining room enjoying the bay scenery while approaching the wharf.

Disembark and say goodbye to the crew.

12.00 - 17.00: Transfer back to Hanoi with a short stop of about 30 minutes for refreshment.

Arrive in Hanoi. End of services.

Meals: Breakfast, Brunch
Accommodation: NA
